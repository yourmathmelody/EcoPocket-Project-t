# Project: EcoPocket (Your Green Score in Your Pocket)

## 1. Project Overview
EcoPocket is a FinTech-based sustainability application designed to track the carbon footprint of individual expenditures and suggest sustainable alternatives.

## 2. The Problem
Users lack immediate awareness of the environmental impact of their purchases and often don't know where to find greener alternatives during their shopping journey.

## 3. MVP (Minimum Viable Product) Scope
- **Expense Entry:** Manual input for expense amount and category.
- **Eco-Score Algorithm:** A mathematical calculation of carbon impact based on category coefficients.
- **Green Alternatives & CTA:** When a high-carbon expense is logged, the system suggests a sustainable alternative brand or habit with a clickable call-to-action (CTA) link.
- **Weekly Progress Dashboard:** Visual summary of the user's weekly "Green Score."

## 4. Target Audience
- Environmentally conscious university students.
- Young professionals looking for sustainable shopping alternatives.

## 5. Success Metrics
- Click-through rate (CTR) on suggested green alternatives.
- Improvement in the user's "Green Score" over time.
